Exploration of Underground Mines for Geologic Research


Gasado
Location: South Korea
The deposit was discovered in 1989 by Ivanhoe geologist Doug Kirwin and his team after they found the remarkable Lighthouse Vein on the south coast
La Independencia
Location: Colombia
The Zancudo Project includes the historic producing Independencia Mine and has a rich history dating back over 250 years
Mugwump
Location: US (California)
A former lode Au occurrence/mine located about 1.6 km (1.0 mile) W of Alleghany, on the S slope of Pliocene Ridge, W of Wet Ravine
Sterling Hill
Location: US (New Jersey)
The predominant minerals were finally identified to be rich in the element zinc, and in 1836 several small companies began mining, triggering quite a few land disputes. In 1897 the New Jersey Zinc Company consolidated the properties and began a major operation for the extraction of zinc
