Gasado Mine, nestled in the heart of South Korea, stands as a significant site for geological research. This mine provides an excellent case study for the application of 3D geologic modeling techniques, thanks to its diverse mineralogy and complex geological structures.

A research at Gasado Mine aims to delve into these geological complexities, employing advanced GIS and modeling technologies to bring to light the subterranean wonders of this location. The insights gained promise to enrich our understanding of geologic formations and mining processes.
